Our Story

About Sweet Revenge

Welcome to Sweet Revenge Bake 'N' Cafe — the beloved neighbourhood bakery right in the heart of Ananthnagar, Electronic City, Bangalore. We've been proudly serving the community with the finest baked goods and comfort food with a warm smile, every single day.

From our signature chocolate donuts to creamy butterscotch cakes, crispy burgers and freshly made sandwiches — everything is crafted with care, quality ingredients and loads of love. Open 9AM to 11PM, 365 days a year.

We're more than just a bakery — we're a neighbourhood institution. Come in, sit down, and let us take good care of you. 🍰
